About

Designer with a keen eye for UX and UI — Currently working as Senior Designer for Worth — Based in The Hague, The Netherlands

Hi, my name is Sander de Leeuw. Living in The Hague (NL) together with my lovely wife and son. Over a period of +7 years i’ve worked as freelancer, in-house and at digital agencies, designing digital products for large corporates, consumer brands, government and startups.

User Experience is key in everything i do. I care deeply about creating functional, meaningful and beautiful interactions. I typically like to be involved in projects from the early start, analysing user needs, discovering opportunities and going from exploring different solutions to getting the final details just right. What excites me about my field of work is the power of design to make abstract ideas tangible and how simple prototypes can work as a catalyst for businesses to improve and innovate. Over the last couple of years i’ve transitioned from interface design to a more strategic design role, helping businesses to get and stay ahead of their market by using Design thinking, organising workshops and facilitating design sprints.

I am always open to explore new ventures and challenges.
If you'd like to chat, feel free to contact me.
